How to Play

Step 1: Flip Cards

Click on any card to flip it and reveal what's underneath.

Step 2: Find Matches

Try to remember the location of each card and find matching pairs.

Step 3: Take Turns

In multiplayer mode, players take turns. If you find a match, you get another turn!

Step 4: Win the Game

The player with the most pairs at the end wins! Try to complete the game in as few moves as possible.
