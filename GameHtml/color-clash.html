How to Play Color Clash

Step 1: Choose Your Color

On your turn, select a color from the palette at the bottom of the screen.

Step 2: Claim Territory

Your color will fill your territory. You can only expand from cells you already own.

Step 3: Strategic Choices

Choose colors that appear frequently next to your territory to maximize expansion.

Step 4: Win the Game

The player who controls the most cells when the grid is filled wins the game!
