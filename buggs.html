Known Issues & Bug Report

Critical Warning

Do not enable the "Animation" setting — doing so can break the app or website.

If you encounter issues:

  • If it breaks the website: please contact us at servers.pixelhavengames@gmail.com so we can help you restore access.
  • If the app crashes: you may need to reinstall it to restore functionality.

We're actively working on resolving this issue. Thank you for your patience!

Settings Panel Not Working

Some settings are currently unresponsive or not saving correctly. You may notice certain toggles or options do nothing when clicked.

Expected fix: Within 1–2 weeks.

Burger Menu Broken on Mobile

The burger menu does not work on phones in portrait mode. As a workaround, rotate your phone to landscape mode — this will reveal the full menu.

Expected fix: In progress, patch coming soon.

Menu Selection Unclear

When using the expanded menu in landscape mode, it's not clear which menu item is currently selected. This can be confusing for navigation.

Expected fix: UI update in the next patch.
